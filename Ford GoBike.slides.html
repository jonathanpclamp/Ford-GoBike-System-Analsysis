Trip Duration¶

Most trips fell between ~3 to ~9 minutes in length. No trips appeared to last less than ~1 minute and at the top end, a small number of trips lasted longer than 50 minutes but not longer than 1 hour (which for the purpose of analysis, 1 hour is the max trip duration used for the scope of the analsysis).

Trip duration start time by day and hour¶

The longest trips appear to be consistently around 03:00.

Shortest trips appear to be around 06:00 - 07:00 when other traffic volumes may be low and increase marginally by 08:00, which we have earlier observed to be peak use volume during weekdays.
